# RexGPT – AI Assistant Brand Concept

Welcome! This repository explores the concept of **RexGPT**, a next-generation AI assistant brand designed for future-focused products and research.

---

## What is RexGPT?

**RexGPT** combines:

- **Rex** → Authority, leadership, reliability  
- **GPT** → Generative AI, automation, intelligence  

Together, it represents a conceptual AI brand that is memorable, authoritative, and aligned with GPT-based solutions.

---

## Use Cases / Applications

- Enterprise AI copilots  
- Decision support systems  
- Research automation tools  
- AI SaaS products  
- Productivity & automation frameworks  

---

## Branding Insights

- Short, clear, and authoritative brand names perform best in AI products  
- RexGPT is designed to be instantly recognizable, culturally familiar, and trustworthy  
- Names like RexGPT signal competence, innovation, and modern AI sophistication
